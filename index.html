For non-locals

For everyone who'll be a tourist in Adelaide, we want you to have a great time!

Our recommendations are:

  • Get along to the Fringe Festival and Garden of Unearthly Delights
  • Scoot down south to McLaren Vale (Samuel's Gorge is the pick!) or north to the Barossa (Hentley Farm's a gem!)
  • Head to the beach! Walk between Grange and Henley, or down south to Port Willunga
  • Eat some food at La Trattoria, Market Street Cafe or anywhere you find in the Central Market
  • Drink beers/wine at the King's Head, 2KW, 5 O'Clock Somewhere or the Wheatsheaf
